About Nazmani

Nazmani Bloodweaver is a Hearthstone card that is no longer playable in the Standard format of the game. During it's peak, players used it's card effect to create 'miracles'; aka huge swing turns that can lead to a two-turn win. Decks built around it were popular and also dominated in championships. Overall this was a technically challenging playstyle that required quick thinking with great payoffs!

Nazmani had many synergies with decks mainly built around the following cards:

  • Palm Reading
  • Desperate Prayer
  • Bless
  • Pscyhe Split
  • Gift of Luminance
  • Ellek Mount
  • Malygos the Spellweaver
